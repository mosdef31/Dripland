Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you ship and how long does it take?

We ship worldwide. Orders typically leave our facility within 1–2 business days. Delivery takes 3–7 business days for most regions, depending on carrier and location.

What is your return policy?

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unworn items in original packaging. Start a return from your order confirmation email or contact support with your Order ID.

How does sizing run?

Most styles run true to size. For a roomier fit, consider half-size up in performance runners. If between sizes, choose the larger option.

How do I care for my shoes?

Spot clean with a soft brush or cloth and mild soap. Air dry only; avoid direct heat. Leather uppers benefit from periodic conditioning.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders are processed quickly. If you need to change or cancel, contact support within 1 hour of purchase; after fulfillment begins, changes may not be possible.
